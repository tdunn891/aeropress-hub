World AeroPress Champs

The World AeroPress Championship is a global coffee-making competition, designed to find the world's best cup of AeroPress coffee.

Each season is comprised of a series regional and national competitions, run by independent hosts across 60 countries, which culminates with the World AeroPress Championship final.

The competition itself is a multi-round elimination tournament. In each round, three competitors will face off against each other, simultaneously brewing a single cup of coffee using the AeroPress brewer. Competitors have 8 minutes in which to prepare, brew, and present their coffee to a panel of three judges.
